Welcome to Visual Trading Backtester!

On this platform you can test out trading strategies on historical market data.

How to use:

  1. Select a stock/stocks to trade with.
  2. Select period in the past when you want to test
  3. Select a trading strategy to determine when to buy or sell stocks
  4. Set your money (initial capital) you would invest in stocks
  5. Click on "Simulate" button to start backtesting.

HINT: You can select more stocks, then the initial capital will be splitted equally between stocks.
